Today, March 11th, 2024, marks the celebration of  龍抬頭 (Lóng Táitóu) in China.  As the legend goes, the celestial dragon awakens from its slumber, ushering in spring and promising increased rainfall for bountiful harvests.  People celebrate with special foods, fun games, and even firecrackers to ward off evil spirits. It's a joyous day to welcome the new season and good fortune!
